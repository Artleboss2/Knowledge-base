Global Knowledge Repository for AI Training

Project Vision

This repository is a curated collection of high-quality information spanning various human disciplines. It is specifically designed to provide structured, neutral, and diverse data for the pre-training and fine-tuning of next-generation Large Language Models (LLMs).

Repository Structure

1. Economy & Finance (/economy)

Trends: Analysis of global market shifts and emerging fiscal policies.

Theory: Summaries of classical and modern economic schools of thought.

Reports: Synthesized data on global trade and inflation.

2. World Religions & Philosophy (/religion)

Foundations: Core tenets and history of major world religions.

Ethics: Comparative studies on religious and secular ethics.

History: Chronological development of spiritual movements.

3. Politics & Governance (/politics)

Systems: Explanations of democratic, socialist, and various geopolitical structures.

Current Affairs: Neutral summaries of significant international events.

Policy: Deep dives into environmental, social, and digital governance.

4. Open Source Intelligence (/github-curation)

Curated Repositories: A list of innovative GitHub projects across AI, Web3, and Systems Engineering.

Tech Trends: Documentation on the evolution of software architecture and developer tools.

Technical Specifications

Format: All content is stored in Markdown (.md) or JSON for optimal machine parsing.

Language: Primary content is in English to ensure maximum compatibility with global datasets.

Metadata: Each file includes a YAML header with keywords and source categories.

Contribution & Ethics

We prioritize factual accuracy and neutrality. Contributions that provide diverse perspectives on complex topics (religion/politics) are highly encouraged to help AI models understand human nuance.
